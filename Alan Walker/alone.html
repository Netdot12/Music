Alone by Alan Walker

"Alone" is a song by Norwegian record producer and DJ Alan Walker. Incorporating uncredited vocals provided by Swedish singer Noonie Bao,[1] it was released commercially for digital download on 2 December 2016.[2] A second part, a collaboration with Ava Max titled "Alone, Pt. II", was released on 27 December 2019. A third part, named Better Of (Alone, Pt. III), featuring Vikkstar123, will be released on 28 September, 2023.[3] The song garnered critical acclaim upon release.
