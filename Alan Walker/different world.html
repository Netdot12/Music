Different World : Alan Walker

"Different World" is a song by Norwegian DJ and producer Alan Walker, K-391 and Sofia Carson featuring Corsak [zh]. It was included on Walker's debut studio album of the same name, Different World, which was released on 14 December 2018.[1] The song is a re-worked version of a track originally known as "Sevje" by K-391.
