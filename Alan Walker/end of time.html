End Of Time : Alan Walker, K391 and Ahrix

"End of Time" is a song by Norwegian music producer K-391, Norwegian DJ Alan Walker and Dutch music producer Ahrix, released as a single on 6 March 2020 through MER Musikk.[1] It contains uncredited vocals provided by Kristin Carpenter.[2]
