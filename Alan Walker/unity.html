Unity : Alan Walker

Unity is a song by Alan Walker that was released in 2019. What set this song apart from the others was that Alan created it with dozens of his fans-Walkers who were passionate about music and producing. All their IDs are listed in the description of the Unity video on YouTube. The song was re-released on Spotify and other music media with a new vocalist in 2022
