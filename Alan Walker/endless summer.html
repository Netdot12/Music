Endless Summer : Alan Walker

"Endless Summer" is a song by Norwegian music producer Alan Walker and Zak Abel. For Walker, summer is the most anticipated season. It's the season where he creates the most memorable moments, meeting various people while traveling and spending time with family and friends. That's why he wanted to create a summer soundtrack that captures this feeling and blissful moments. Zak Abel's vocals nailed it and translated it all perfectly. I hope you all enjoy "Endless Summer" just like I do!
